no longer

a few days

e.g

millions of lives

With the help of …

借助于,利用,在…的帮助下

e.g

With the help of EXPLAIN,you can see where you should add indexes to tables.

借助于EXPLAIN,你可以做….

Information gathering has been made easier with the help of the Internet.
在互联网的帮助下，收集信息变得更加容易。

I read English magazines with the help of a dictionary.
我借助字典翻阅英文杂志.

He made himself understood with the help of gestures.
他比画着讲.
